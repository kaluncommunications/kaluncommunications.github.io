About Us

Kalun Communications Inc. provides a full service to companies in obtaining Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) tax credits from the federal government for technological development. Our staff is experienced in electrical engineering and information technology (IT) development in Canada.

We assist companies in defending their applications, audit reviews, or appeals. We represent companies as the SR&ED expert/material witness in audit meetings, file Notices of Objections (NOO), and appeal disallowed claims in tax court.

Our principal, Paul Wong, has worked in the SR&ED Tax Credit Program for Canada Revenue Agency as a research and technology advisor, manager, and also as an outside consultant for project evaluations, appeals and court cases.

We have been a communications equipment manufacturer in Ontario since 1985, developing hardware and software for communications, wireless, electronics, IT, and solar energy, with a line of successful products developed for sale and claimed for SR&ED tax credits.
