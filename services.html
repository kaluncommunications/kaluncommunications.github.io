SR&ED Claims and Appeals

You may be eligible to receive Canadian federal government tax credits for Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) if your work involves creating new or improving existing materials, devices, software, products or processes for your business. Canadian corporations and sole proprietors can apply.

Please call us to determine if your project or work is eligible for SR&ED tax credits. The first meeting is free.

How we can help

  1. In claiming SR&ED tax credits

    We advise in SR&ED eligibility, prepare your claim, and defend your claim as an SR&ED expert. Having worked in CRA in the SR&ED program for many years, our professional staff will assist your project claims by providing:

    • a better coverage of eligible activities in your claim
    • accurate interpretations of your activities according to the SR&ED criteria and policy on eligibility to improve your chances of eligibility

  2. In disapproved claims

    We act as the third party witness in appeals for claims that were disallowed by the CRA. We are experienced in preparing Notices of Objections (NOO) to the CRA and appealing disallowed claims in tax court.
